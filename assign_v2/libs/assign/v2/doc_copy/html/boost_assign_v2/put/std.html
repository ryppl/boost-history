#include <boost/assign/v2/put/std.hpp>

Description

Specializations of put_aux::modifier<>, and an associated keyword, whose generic identifier is denoted _tag, that meet requirements codified below.

Concept

Identifier mf denotes a member function of cont that increases the size of cont by one.

Example
