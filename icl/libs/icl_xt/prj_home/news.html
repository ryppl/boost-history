What's New?

[11-10-2010] The Interval Container Library, ICL, formerly known as Interval Template Library, ITL, is now prepared for inclusion into the next boost release 1.46 and has been moved to the Boost trunk. The issues raised during the formal review are mainly solved or imple­mented as agreed in the discussion.

[04-18-2010] The Interval Template Library, ITL, has been accepted into Boost! Many thanks to all the reviewers and review manager Hartmut Kaiser.
