Boost.Smart Pointer

Acknowledgments

Boost.Smart Pointer began life as Loki::SmartPtr, the brainchild of Andrei Alexandrescu. As such, basically the entirety of the design and a good deal of the implementation are his original work. It was modified by David B. Held to conform to the Boost style and coding conventions (as well as integrate with existing Boost facilities). Changes and enhancements beyond the original Loki design were also implemented by David.

Gennadiy Rozental offered extensive comments and suggestions, having implemented another variant of Loki::SmartPtr.

Documentation and test programs were written entirely by David Held, with concepts taken from Modern C++ Design, as appropriate. The Boost HTML Documentation Templates by William Kempf were used to produce the documentation. Thanks to Bill for the excellent templates!

Other people who have made comments or contributed suggestions to this library are (in alphabetical order): David Abrahams, Giovanni Bajo, Greg Colvin, Beman Dawes, Peter Dimov, and Phil Nash. Apologies to anyone I may have missed.
