Boost.IndexedSet Acknowledgements

Fernando Cacciola, Darren Cook, Beman Dawes, Jeremy Maitin-Shepard and Daryle Walker from the Boost mailing list provided useful suggestions for improvement on the first alpha releases of the library. Gang Wang discovered several bugs in the code.

Pavel Voženílek has been immensely helpful in thoroughly reviewing every single bit of the library, and he also suggested several extra functionalities, most notably range querying, safe mode and polymorphic key extractors. Thank you!

Boost.IndexedSet could not have been written without Aleksey Gurtovoy et. al. superb Boost MPL Library. Also, Aleksey's techniques for dealing with ETI-related problems in MSVC++ 6.0 helped solve some internal issues of the library.

The internal implementation of red-black trees is based on that of SGI STL stl_tree.h file:
